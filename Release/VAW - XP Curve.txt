Re-balances the XP curve of the game: all levels are now 1000 XP.
 - Quests now grant more XP in general.
 - Exploration activities, such as discovering areas, scanning flora, fauna and planets now grant more XP.
 - Temple puzzle now grants more XP.
 - Crafting, cooking and research no longer grant XP.
 - Lockpicking now grants more XP.
 - Speech challenges now grant more XP.
